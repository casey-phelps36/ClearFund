# Privacy Policy for ClearFund

**Effective Date:** 5/4/2026

ClearFund respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the ClearFund mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### a. Personal Information
- Email address (for account creation and login)
- User authentication data via Firebase Authentication

### b. Financial Information
- Income data
- Expense data
- Budget categories and financial inputs entered by the user

### c. Device and Usage Information
- Device type, operating system, and app usage data
- Analytics data (via Firebase Analytics)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the ClearFund app
- Display personalized financial summaries and insights
- Authenticate users and manage accounts
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Monitor and analyze usage trends

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do **not sell your personal data**.

We may share your data with the following third-party services:
- **Firebase (Google LLC)** for authentication, database storage, analytics, and app functionality

These services may process data on our behalf in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:
- Secure authentication using Firebase Authentication
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Restricted access to stored data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

---


## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Update or correct your data

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

ClearFund is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on our website with an updated effective date.

---


## 8. Compliance with Google Play Requirements

This Privacy Policy is provided in accordance with Google Play Developer policies and discloses how ClearFund collects, uses, and shares user data.
